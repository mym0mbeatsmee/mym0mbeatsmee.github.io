Gokarna Mahabaleshwar Temple

History

Gokarna, a city blessed with serene beaches and renowned temples, is a place of spiritual significance. Among its sacred sites, the Gokarna Mahabaleshwar Temple holds a special place, drawing numerous pilgrims. This temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is not only steeped in local lore but also features in the ancient epics, the Ramayana and the Mahabharata. Often referred to as Dakshin Kashi, it is regarded with the same reverence as the holy city of Kashi.
